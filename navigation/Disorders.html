    Anixety Disorders

    Agoraphobia

    This is a condition where you experience intense fear,worry,or panic in public places.Feelings that something dangerous might happen.



    Panic Disorder

    This occurs with a surge of intense fear that strikes suddenly and repeatedly without warning.



    Seperation Anixety Disorder

    This is an anxiety disorder that pretains to a fear or seperation from one's home,family,and friends.



    Phobias

    Specific phobia are intense fears.Phobias are an intense fear about an object or situation.



    Social Anxiety Disorder

    Social Anixety comes from fear involving social situations.



    Generalized Anxiety Disorder

    GAD is a type of disorder that's chronic,involving excessive worry and tension even when there's nothing to provoke it.

    This is also known as Dysthmia.This is constantlys being depressed throughout your everyday life.

    Per/Post partum Disorder

    Depression that comes before and after you have given birth.

    Premenstraul Dysphoric Disorder

    Feeling irritable,depressed.or anxious a week or two before your period.

    Seasonal affective disorder

    Mood Disorder that occurs in climates that have less sunlight at certain times of the year.

    Situational Depression

    This is depression that can occur after something traumatic happens.

    Major Depressive Disorder

    A constant depressive modd or loss of interest in activities.

    Periods of over-active and excited behaviour that can have a significant impact on your life.
