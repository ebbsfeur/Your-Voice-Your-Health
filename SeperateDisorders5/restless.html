Major Depressive Disorders

    Overview

    Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest.It affects how you feel, think and behave and can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems. You may have trouble doing normal day-to-day activities, and sometimes you may feel as if life isn't worth living. More than just a bout of the blues, depression isn't a weakness and you can't simply "snap out" of it.

    Symptoms

  • Feeling of sadness,tearfulness,or emptiness
  • Angry outburst ,Easily irritable
  • Loss of interest in everyday activities
  • Reduce apeptite and weight loss
  • Have trouble falling asleep or staying asleep
  • Trouble Foucsing and remembering
  • Physical Aches and Pains

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Call 911 or a Hotline (988)if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Health Habits
  • Support Group
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
  • Communicate with People you trust
