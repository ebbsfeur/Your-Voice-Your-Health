Cyclothymic Disorders

    Overview

    Cyclothymic Disorders are a rare mood disorder. This causes emotional ups and downs throughout your everyday life. One minute you might feel on top of the world the next you are feeling down with no energy to do anything. Even though this is not as extreme as bipolar disorder you still need to seek help to manage these symptoms. This can be passed down by genetics or environmental issues.

    Symptoms (Highs of Cyclothymic)

  • Exaggerated feeling of happiness
  • Extreme optimism
  • Inflated Self-Esteem
  • Talking more then Usual
  • Racing Thoughts
  • Excessive physical activity
  • Decreased need for sleep
  • Easily Distracted

    • Symptoms (Lows of Cyclothymic)

  • Feeling Sad or Hopeless
  • Easily Irritated
  • Feeling Worthless
  • Fatigued or feeling slowed down
  • Suicidial Thoughts

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Call 911 or a Hotline if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Avoid Alcohol
  • Avoid Drugs
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
