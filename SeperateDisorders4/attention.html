Additional specifiers for bipolar disorder

    Overview

    Agoraphobia is the fear of being in sitautions escaping is difficult. Most people think it is just a fear of open spaces , however, it is really can be a fear of traveling,visiting public areas or even just simply leaving your house. This can develop as a complication of panic disorders and anxiety disorders.

    Symptoms

  • Rapid Heart Beat
  • Rapid Breathing
  • Feeling Hot or Sweaty
  • Feeling Sick
  • Panic Attack
  • Avoiding leaving your house

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Regular Exercise Routines
  • Eating Healthier
  • Avoid Alcohol
  • Avoid Drugs
  • Avoid Caffeine(ex.coffee,tea,and cola)
  • If a panic attack occurs take death breaths
    ,and focus on something that brings you happiness.
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
