Manic Episodes

    Overview

    Hympomanic Episodes is a condition which you go through a time of abnormally elevated,extreme changes in your mood or emotions,energy level or activity level.This is usually followed by depression right afterwards of having an episode.

    Symptoms

  • Have an abnormally high level of acivity or energy
  • Feeling extremely happy excited
  • Showing impulsive behavior thats harmful
  • Fast Talking
  • Have trouble falling asleep or staying asleep
  • Psychosis

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Call 911 or a Hotline if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Health Habits
  • Support Group
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
  • Communicate with People you trust
  • Learn When you are Having a Anxiety Attack
  • If you feel a Anxiety Attack focus and slowly breath
