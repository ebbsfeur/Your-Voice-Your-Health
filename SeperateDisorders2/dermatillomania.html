Dermatillomania

    Overview

    This start seven to ten days before your period starts and continues a few days into your period. This is when you experience intense mood swings and emotions before your period starts. This can put a tole on your relastionships and daily life.

    Symptoms

  • Sadness and hopelessness
  • bloating and Tender Breast
  • Anxiety and Tension
  • Extreme moodiness
  • Easily Irritated

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Call 911 or a Hotline (call or text 988)if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Nurition Supplements
  • Diet and Life Changes
  • Herbal Remedies
  • Prescribed Medication
  • Communicate with People you trust
  • Birth Control
  • Antidepressants
