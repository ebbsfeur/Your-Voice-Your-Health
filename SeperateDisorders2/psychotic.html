    Overview

    Mixed Feautures is when you experience manic and deppressive episodes at the same time. You will feel high and low symptoms as the same time in a single episode.This is commonly in people with bipolar disorder.

    Symptoms

  • Have an abnormally high level of acivity or energy
  • Feeling extremely happy excited
  • Showing impulsive behavior thats harmful
  • Display purposeless
  • Have trouble falling asleep or staying asleep
  • Worrying about everyday life or work

  • Call 911 or a Hotline if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Health Habits
  • Support Group
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
