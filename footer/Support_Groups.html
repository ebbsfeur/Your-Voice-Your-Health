Mental Health

Mental Health is something that alot of people look over.Mental Health is dealing with our emotional, phsycological , and social well being.Mental health plays a big part on how we interact everyday. Most people don't know how to address mental health.Everyone says college is easy and fun , however, no one tells you how mentally draining school can be. Most college students are afraid to speak out or open up to people about their mental health.This website allows you to explore different disorders and provide resources to help you deal with the symptoms of challenges of mental health everyday. You're not alone we are here to support you.
