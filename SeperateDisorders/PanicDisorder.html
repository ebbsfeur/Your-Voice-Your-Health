Panic Disorders

    Overview

    People with panic disorder have frequent and unexpected panic attacks. These attacks are characterized by a sudden wave of fear or a sense of losing control even when there is no clear danger or trigger. Untreated this can lead to a difficulty in you life with work and school. They can run in families but know one knows for sure where they originate from ,however, reasearch has shown that this can come from certain parts of your brain and biological processess.

    Symptoms

  • Pounding or racing heart
  • Sweating and Chills
  • Trembling and Difficulty Breathing
  • A fear or avoidance of places where panic attacks have occurred in the past

    • Treatments / Tips

  • Call 911 or a Hotline if you are having Suicidial thoughts
  • Routine Doctor Visits
  • Health Habits
  • Support Group
  • Therapy
  • Prescribed Medication
  • Communicate with People you trust
  • Learn When you are Having a Anxiety Attack
  • If you feel a Anxiety Attack focus and slowly breath
